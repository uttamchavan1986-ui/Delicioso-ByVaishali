Our Story

Baked with Heart

Welcome to Delicioso — a passionate home bakery nestled in the heart of Hulimavu, Bengaluru. We believe every celebration deserves something truly special: made fresh, made with love, and made just for you.

Specialising in custom cakes for every occasion and handcrafted chocolates from the finest ingredients — every Delicioso creation is baked fresh to order, right from our home kitchen. No mass production, no shortcuts. Just pure, wholesome goodness crafted over 4.5 years.
